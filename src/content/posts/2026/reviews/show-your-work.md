Austin Kleon makes the case for sharing your process, not just your finished products.

## Key Ideas

- Share something small every day
- You don't have to be an expert to teach
- Build a "scenius" (community of peers)

## Application

This book directly inspired how I approach this portfolio—sharing both finished projects and works in progress.
