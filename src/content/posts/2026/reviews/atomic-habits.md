James Clear's framework for habit formation has fundamentally changed how I approach personal growth.

## Key Takeaways

1. **1% better every day** - Small improvements compound over time
2. **Identity-based habits** - Focus on who you want to become
3. **Make it obvious, attractive, easy, and satisfying** - The four laws of behavior change

## Why It's Evergreen

This book provides a systems-based approach to habits that applies universally. Whether you're trying to exercise more, read more, or build any skill, the framework works.
