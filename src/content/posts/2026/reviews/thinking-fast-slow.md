Nobel laureate Daniel Kahneman reveals the fascinating—and often flawed—ways our minds work.

## The Two Systems

- **System 1**: Fast, intuitive, emotional
- **System 2**: Slow, deliberate, logical

## Why This Matters

Understanding cognitive biases helps in everything from software design to personal finance decisions.
