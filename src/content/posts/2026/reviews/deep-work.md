Cal Newport argues that deep work—the ability to focus without distraction—is becoming increasingly rare and valuable.

## Core Concepts

- **Deep work vs. shallow work** - Not all tasks are created equal
- **Attention residue** - Why context switching kills productivity
- **Rituals and routines** - Creating conditions for flow

## Implementation

I've adopted a time-blocking approach based on this book, dedicating mornings to deep work and afternoons to shallow tasks.
