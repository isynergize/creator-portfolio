Don Norman's masterpiece on why some designs are intuitive and others are infuriating.

## Timeless Principles

- **Affordances** - What actions are possible
- **Signifiers** - What signals communicate possible actions
- **Feedback** - Confirming that an action occurred

## Still Relevant

Written in 1988, updated in 2013, and still the most important book on design fundamentals.
