These classic chocolate chip cookies are a crowd favorite. The secret is using both granulated and brown sugar for the perfect texture.

1. **Preheat** your oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. **Mix dry ingredients**: Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Set aside.

3. **Cream butter and sugars**: Beat the softened butter with both sugars until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes.

4. **Add eggs and vanilla**: Beat in the eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract.

5. **Combine**: Gradually mix in the flour mixture until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.

6. **Scoop and bake**: Drop rounded tablespoons onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake for 9-12 minutes until golden brown.

7. **Cool**: Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

## Tips

- Don't overmix the dough after adding flour
- For thicker cookies, chill the dough for 30 minutes before baking
- Use a mix of milk and dark chocolate chips for depth
